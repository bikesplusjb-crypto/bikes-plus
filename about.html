About Bikes Plus

Bikes Plus is the Treasure Coast's one-stop bicycle shop and mobile repair, right here in Jensen Beach.

Whether you're chasing a new personal best, cruising the beach, or just getting back on two wheels, we've got the bikes, parts, and know-how to keep you rolling. We carry most major brands, service everything from vintage steel to the newest electric bikes, and back every repair with our parts and labor guarantee.

And if you can't make it to the shop, we'll come to you — our mobile repair service covers the Treasure Coast, so a flat tire or a tune-up never has to wreck your week.
